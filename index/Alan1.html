Panels from Babylon Alan Clark
My work goes back and forth between a dystopia and utopian ideals. I dont believe in a utopia where everything is perfect and serene --I think we have to claim, take action, free, and build our utopias. These panels are building around freeing prisoners in Babylon, bringing them to an underground society.
