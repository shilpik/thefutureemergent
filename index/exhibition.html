Featuring ARTISTS: Amy Balkin, Lonny Brooks, Kevin Cooley, Alan Clark, David Huffman, Serena Elston, Alicia Escott, Delta_Ark, Tiare Ribeaux, Malik Seneferu, Jody Stillwater, The Bureau for Linguistical Reality, Kytana Winn, and Connie Zheng
Works by 12 artists suggest hints of sustenance as we live the unimaginable and seek radical possibility for the future. Weaving multiple strands, from an Afrofuturist vision to the algae fields of Hawaii to new seeds pods in Northern California, we operate within the realm of speculative visioning: changing the way we experience the present by seeing present as past and practicing the future we want.
To keep us all safe from COVID-19, we have decided to pull back on gallery visits for now.
